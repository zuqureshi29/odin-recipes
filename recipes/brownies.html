Brownies

Description

These rich, fudgy brownies are a chocolate lover’s dream — soft in the center with a perfectly crisp top. Made with melted butter, cocoa powder, and a touch of vanilla, they deliver that classic homemade flavor you can’t resist. The addition of walnut halves adds a satisfying crunch that balances the sweetness beautifully.

Simple ingredients come together in minutes to create a decadent dessert that’s perfect for any occasion. Whether served warm with a scoop of ice cream or packed for a quick treat, these brownies are sure to become your go-to recipe whenever a chocolate craving strikes.

Ingredients

  • baking spray
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup walnut halves

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan with baking spray.
  3. Whisk sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Decorate with walnut halves.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until top is crinkled and a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  7. Let cool completely in the pan on a wire rack before slicing into squares. Enjoy!
