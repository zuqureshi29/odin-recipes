Fettucine Alfredo

Description

This creamy Alfredo pasta is the ultimate comfort dish — rich, velvety, and bursting with flavor. Made with butter, cream, and freshly grated Parmesan, the sauce coats every strand of pasta perfectly for a silky, indulgent bite. Garlic adds a subtle depth, while a sprinkle of black pepper brings everything together.

Perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions, this Alfredo recipe is simple yet elegant. Pair it with grilled chicken, shrimp, or roasted vegetables for a satisfying meal that feels restaurant-quality but comes together easily at home.

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
  4. Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
